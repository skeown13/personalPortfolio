ABOUT ME

I did not originally start off in the Software Engineering Field. Before I began my journey into Software Engineering I attended California State University - Stanislaus studying Criminal Justice - Concentrating in Forensic Science, where I obtained my Bachelor of the Arts Degree. While I was there, I was able to do an internship with the Stockton Police Department's Crime Scene Investigation Unit, which was such an amazing experience! But I realized that it was not what I wanted to do with the rest of my life.

So here I am now a full fledged Software Engineer. Though I didn't just become one overnight. My journey here began with myself self-studying while working the Front Desk at The Marina del Rey Hotel. From there I got my AA from Santa Monica College in Computer Programming. While going there I focused on disciplines such as Visual Basic, PHP, and SQL. About a year after graduating SMC I attended CodeSmith. CodeSmith is a 3 month, day-in day-out intensive deep-dive learning program into the JavaScript and React architectures; overall the MERN Stack. While there I got to deep dive into React Native; being a part of a 4 person team that fleshed out a full front end for a lady that was creating an application for both iOS and Android platforms.

Now I have the distinct honor of being a Teaching Assistant, which has allowed me to be able to impart the knowledge that I have learned to others that are taking a similar Journey. And while taking this Journey with these new and aspiring Software Engineers, my Journey continues as does my knowledge and abilities. I have worked directly with the renowned Universities, UCLA and UC Berkley. As well as working indirectly with many other Universities across the United States and further; by grading assignments completed by students all around the world and even getting the opportunity to be some of these student's Tutor. So far my life has been such an amazing Journey and I can't wait to see where it leads next. I am open to ALL opportunities!
